Introduction

We want our legacy to be more than an app.

The San Antonio team is focusing its efforts on a few core areas that build on long-running work done by the city and community to push themselves into the 21st century.

Our engagements are more than apps -- some focus on building policies to sustain the civic hacking movement in the future or streamlining paper processes to make it easier for everyone to get things done.

Our big-picture goal is to empower residents to own the city they live in, and to give residents the opportunity to give fearless feedback and fix community problems themselves. In partnership with the City of San Antonio, we also want to help the city continue its efforts to be more transparent and modernize its internal technology processes.
